#RADDCHAT

Michael Facemire, Principal Analyst serving Application Development and Delivery Professionals, joined us for a Tweet Jam in December.

This Tweet Jam explored the myths and realities of a RAD platform in today’s digital world. Participants in the chat interacted directly with Michael regarding past or current research, industry trends, and best practices.

A few topics explored during the Tweet Jam were:

  • Why RAD has really matured in the age of digital business?
  • What front-ends are leading in mobile?
  • Why backends are so important for mobile app dev?
  • Can a RAD platform really fit into a devops strategy?
  • How important is UX for the adoption of mobile apps?
  • And more...
