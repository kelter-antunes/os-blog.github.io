Rapid Application Delivery (RAD) Platforms - Myths versus Reality

We are hosting Forrester Research for a Tweet Jam featuring Michael Facemire, Principal Analyst serving Application Development and Delivery Professionals. This online, live conversation will happen Wednesday, December 9th at 1:00 pm eastern.  

This tweet jam will explore the myths and realities of a RAD platform in today’s digital world. Participants in the chat can ask questions directly to Michael regarding past or current research, industry trends, and best practices.

A few topics that will be explored during the Tweet Jam:

  • Why RAD has really matured in the age of digital business?
  • What front-ends are leading in mobile?
  • Why backends are so important for mobile app dev?
  • Can a RAD platform really fit into a devops strategy?
  • How important is UX for the adoption of mobile apps?
  • And more...          

A tweet jam is a one-hour moderated discussion on Twitter to answer questions on relevant issues. The tweet jam will be led by a moderator with our guest Michael supplying answers to keep the discussion flowing. OutSystems experts will be available and everyone is welcome to join the conversation on Twitter by following the hashtag #RADDCHAT and @outsystems at the time of the event.

Michael has more than 13 years of experience in the software development industry. He joined Forrester from IBM, where he delivered industry-leading applications in mobile, social software, portal, and workflow. He is a leading expert on mobile software development, software development process, and next-generation software architectures.

Guidelines for participants:

  • Join the chat and introduce yourself
  • Include the hashtag #RADDCHAT in each tweet
  • Please use the jam to have an informative dialogue not individual product/service promotion
  • Keep your tweets focused on the topic
  • Questions? Contact Ann Conrad at ann.conrad@outsystems.com

We hope you will join us for an insightful conversation with Michael!
